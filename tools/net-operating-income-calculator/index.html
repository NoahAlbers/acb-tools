These are NOT operating expenses — leave them out
  • Mortgage payments (debt service). NOI measures the property, not your loan. Two investors can buy the same building with different financing — NOI stays identical so deals stay comparable.
  • Capital expenditures (capex). A new roof or HVAC is a long-term investment in the asset, not a recurring cost of operating it. Lenders and appraisers exclude it (budget for it separately).
  • Depreciation. It's a paper deduction for taxes, not cash leaving your pocket. NOI tracks real operating cash.
  • Income taxes. Your personal tax bill depends on your bracket, entity, and other income — nothing to do with how the property performs.
